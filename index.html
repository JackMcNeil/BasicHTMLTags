Pardon My Take

Presented by Barstool Sports

Pardon My Take is one of the biggest sports podcast in the world in terms of listeners. This podcast is hosted by Dan "Big Cat" Katz and PFT Commentator. This Podcast was started in 2016. The Podcast averages about 750 thousand to 1.5 million listeners per episode.


The format of the show is to first talk about the most recent sporting events that happend, or big news stories (avoiding politics). Then they bring on a guest for an interview, this can range from an actor, an athelete, or their co-workers dads. The latter half of the show are segements that they do, more on that later. Then they finish the show with a remix of Take on Me by A-ha.

Segments

  • Fyre Fest of the Week
  • Guys on Chicks
  • Mount Rushmore
  • Who's Back of the week
  • Hot Seat / Cool Throne
